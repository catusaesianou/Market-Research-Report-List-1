<p><p>The high-end inertial sensors market comprises various types, including accelerometers, gyroscopes, magnetometers, GNSS receivers, and others. Accelerometers measure linear acceleration, while gyroscopes detect rotational motion. Magnetometers gauge magnetic fields, providing orientation data. GNSS receivers enable precise positioning and navigation using satellite signals. Each type plays a vital role in applications such as aerospace, automotive, robotics, and consumer electronics, enhancing functionalities like stability control, navigation, and motion tracking, driving demand for advanced sensor technologies in diverse industries.</p></p>
