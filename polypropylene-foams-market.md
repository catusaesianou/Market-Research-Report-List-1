<p><p>The polypropylene foams market consists of two main types: expanded polypropylene foams (EPP) and extruded polypropylene foams (XPP). Expanded polypropylene foams are lightweight, resilient, and offer excellent insulation properties, making them ideal for packaging, automotive, and construction applications. In contrast, extruded polypropylene foams are produced through a continuous extrusion process, providing uniform thickness and surface finish. They are commonly used in applications requiring higher density and specific mechanical properties, such as insulation and cushioning in various industries.</p></p>
