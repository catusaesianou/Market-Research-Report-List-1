<p><strong>The Cryoablation Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point</li><li>Multielectrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cryoablation catheters market is categorized into two main types: single point and multielectrode catheters. Single point catheters deliver localized cryogenic energy to specific target tissues, often utilized for precise ablation procedures in conditions like arrhythmias. In contrast, multielectrode catheters feature multiple cryo-ablative sites, allowing for broader treatment areas and simultaneous ablation of multiple points, enhancing efficiency. Both types cater to diverse clinical needs, improving outcomes in minimally invasive surgeries and expanding treatment options for patients.</p></p>
