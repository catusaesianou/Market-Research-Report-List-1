<p><strong>The Self-Cleaning Photocatalytic Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homogeneous Photocatalysis</li><li>Heterogeneous Photocatalysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-cleaning photocatalytic coatings market is categorized into homogeneous and heterogeneous photocatalysis. Homogeneous photocatalysis involves a uniform distribution of photocatalysts within the medium, leading to effective reactions but often requiring precise conditions. In contrast, heterogeneous photocatalysis employs solid catalysts that interact with pollutants, allowing easier separation and reuse. Both types leverage light energy to activate chemical reactions that decompose organic materials and pollutants, facilitating self-cleaning properties on surfaces and enhancing hygiene and maintenance in various applications.</p></p>
