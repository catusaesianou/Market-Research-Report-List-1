<p><p>The Thermoplastic Polyurethane Rubber (TPU) market is primarily categorized into three types: polyester-based, polyether-based, and others. Polyester-based TPUs are known for their excellent abrasion resistance and high mechanical strength, making them ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Polyether-based TPUs offer superior hydrolysis resistance and flexibility, making them suitable for footwear and medical products. The "others" category includes customized formulations and blends that cater to specific performance requirements, expanding the versatility and applications of TPU in various industries.</p></p>
