<p><p>The Non-Invasive Glucose Monitoring market consists of two primary types: Wearable Blood Glucose Monitoring Systems and Table Top Blood Glucose Monitoring Systems. Wearable systems are designed for continuous tracking, integrating sensors within devices like smartwatches or fitness bands, offering real-time glucose levels without painful finger pricks. In contrast, Table Top systems are typically larger, standalone units that can provide glucose readings quickly when a small blood sample is placed on a testing strip, catering to both home and clinical settings.</p></p>
