<p><p>The car drive system market is primarily segmented into three types: Front-Wheel Drive (FWD), Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD), and All-Wheel Drive/Four-Wheel Drive (AWD/4WD). FWD vehicles drive the front wheels, offering better fuel efficiency and traction in adverse conditions, suitable for city driving. RWD vehicles power the rear wheels, providing improved handling and performance, ideal for sports and luxury cars. AWD/4WD systems distribute power to all wheels, enhancing off-road capability and traction, making them popular in SUVs and trucks.</p></p>
