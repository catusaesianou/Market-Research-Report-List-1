<p><p>E-bicycle hub motors are categorized into two main types: rear motors and front motors. Rear hub motors are installed in the wheel's rear, providing better traction and a balanced ride, making them popular for commuting and trekking. They excel in hill climbing and offer more power. In contrast, front hub motors are mounted in the front wheel, delivering a lighter weight distribution and easier handling, ideal for casual riders. Both types enhance the overall riding experience, catering to different cycling preferences.</p></p>
